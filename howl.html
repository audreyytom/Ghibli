Howl's Moving Castle

The Witch of the Waste curses young Sophie Hatter, turning her into an elderly crone. She runs towards the hills, where a moving castle prowls, ashamed of how she seems. It is rumoured to be the property of the notoriously dishonest young and attractive wizard Howl. Sophie makes an acquaintance with the fire demon Calcifer inside the castle, who assures her that he can make her youthful once more. She must assist Calcifer in breaking free from Howl, but Calcifer cannot explain how. Sophie decides to stay, though, and try to learn more about the contract in other ways. Howl, who unlike Calcifer can sense that Sophie is under a spell, nevertheless falls in love with her for who she is and not for what she looks like. Sophie manages to bring life to the moving castle, and she helps Howl to face his former tutor, Madame Suliman.
