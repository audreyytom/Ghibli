The Wind Rises

Jiro is motivated by the renowned Italian aviation designer Caproni in his ambitions of flying and creating stunning aircraft. Jiro, who was born with nearsightedness and was therefore unable to fly, joined a significant Japanese engineering firm in 1927 and went on to become one of the most creative and competent aeroplane designers in the entire world. A large portion of his life is depicted in the movie, along with significant historical occurrences including the Great Kanto Earthquake of 1923, the Great Depression, the TB epidemic, and Japan's entry into World War II. Jiro meets Nahoko, falls in love, and develops a close connection with his work colleague Honjo.
