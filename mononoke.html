Princess Mononoke

Ashitaka, a self-assured teenage warrior, suffers a fatal curse while defending his community from a rampaging boar-god/demon. He must travel to the western forests in order to escape death. Once there, he finds himself caught up in a terrible war that people were waging against the forest. The valiant young woman Princess Mononoke, who was nurtured by a wolf-god, and the forest gods are attacked by the ambitious Lady Eboshi and her devoted clan using firearms. Ashitaka attempts to stop the bloodbath by seeing the positive aspects of both sides. Both sides react negatively to him since they each believe he is backing the adversary.
